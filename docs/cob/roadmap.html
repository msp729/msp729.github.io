At present, I am still in the design phase of Cob's creation.

The overall plan is to define how the language should behave, syntax included, first.

Next, I'll begin working on the compiler- lexer, parser, and translator being the components of a GCC frontend.

Then, I'll make a standard library for Cob. It'll include FS access, collections, numeric classes, etc.

At that point, I'll finally be semi-done with Cob, and I'll begin iterating.


I'll look for flaws in the specification, make a new version of the language, rinse and repeat.
